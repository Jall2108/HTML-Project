Panda Relief works with more than 20 villages within China, such as Liziba village and many more such as Dujiangyan Panda Base, Chengdu Giant Panda Breeding Centre, but our main partner is Liziba village as it is a popular area for pandas to live.

We help fight against deforestation and the destruction of their habitat.

The International Panda Festival in Beijing has been postponed indefinitely following the outbreak of Covid-19.

We do the best to improve living conditions for pandas across China and Asia.

About Panda Relief

Panda Relief is a Irish-based international relief and conservation charity established in 2017 to combat the ongoing threat against pandas and prevent them going extinct.
