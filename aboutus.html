About Us

Panda Relief is a Irish-based international relief and conservation charity established in 2017. To combat the ongoing threat against pandas and prevent them going extinct.

Pandas are very important to the ecosystem as they help to keep mountain forests healthy by spreading seeds in their droppings. We aim to prevent panda extinction and preserve their place in nature.

To help them, we:

  • Help fight against deforestation and the destruction of their habitat.
  • Provide pandas with more nature reserves in their native countries i.e China.
  • Sponsor local villages to take care of the environment.
  • Fund campaigns for the governments to create stricter poaching laws.
  • Encourage panda reproduction.
  • Subsidise bamboo farming.
  • Spread word about pandas and their predicaments

Transparency

Panda Relief is a non profit charity. The charity strives to maintain transparency and accountability.

All donated funds are monitored and reviewed by scholars; funds are spent correctly.

The charity also has a full and transparent audit trail from the donor to the beneficiary.

Please also note that Panda Relief always issues a receipt for a donation. Please always ask for a receipt if you hand over any money to a representative, official or otherwise, of Panda Relief.
